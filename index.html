What do you think is your LIKELY headshot chance when using this weapon?

Why do we ask this?

The Division has a funny way of calculating your headshot chance by taking your 'Accuracy' stat and using that as your headshot chance. Since this doesn't make much sense, we let you input it here to better reflect your weapon, playstyle and personal thumb-skill.

Where to find these numbers

Weapon Dmg
You can find this value on your character stats sheet under 'Weapon Dmg'. Note: This is NOT your DPS. This is a different number so watch out for this one!
Per bullet dmg
You can find this value buy shooting an enemy non-armoured, non-elite NPC in the chest from your optimal range (or closer).
Firearms buff
You can find this value under the DPS value listed on your character sheet.
Weapon Dmg (Magazine)
You can find this value by looking at the ‘All Weapons Damage Increase’ stat on your character stats sheet
Gloves Dmg Buff
You can find this value by looking at any weapon damage gained to SPECIFIC weapon types (e.g Shotguns, Assault Rifles, etc.) in your character stats sheet.
Crit Chance
You can find this value on your character stats sheet.
Crit Bonus Dmg
You can find this value on your character stats sheet.
Accuracy
You can find this value on your character stats sheet.
Headshot Bonus Dmg
You can find this value on your character stats sheet.
RPM
You can find this value by inspecting your currently equipped weapon.
Reload time
You can find this value on your character stats sheet.
Magazine Size
You can find this value by inspecting your currently equipped weapon.
